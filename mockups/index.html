Harmony · a major release

Choose your light

Harmony has always been one warm terracotta on cream. This release lets a person pick the mood they open into. The headline is Mango Sunshine — a warm, golden, sunflower yellow that should make you smile the moment the app opens. Below: seven explorations of that yellow to choose from, then complementary themes (with terracotta kept as the default). Every theme here is one small set of colour tokens, so adding or tuning one later is a few lines, and a quick toggle or feature flag can gate them.

The headline · 7 explorations

Mango Sunshine

“A warm welcoming yellow, cream palette. A sunshine yellow. Something that makes you happy when you open the app, a nice warm golden yellow that makes the app pop, like a sunflower.” The yellow carries the joy through petals, buttons and the done-state; text stays a deep warm cocoa so everything is readable on cream.

Complementary · the supporting cast

The other themes

Candidates for the remaining slots alongside Mango Sunshine. Terracotta is the current look and stays the default. Indigo Night shows the same token system handling a dark theme, so the palette can grow in any direction later.
