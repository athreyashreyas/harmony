Choose your light

Every preview below is built from the app's real colour tokens and the real Home layout, so a theme here is what the app actually becomes. A theme reskins the chrome — background, surfaces, text, and the brand accent (the bloom's heart, the buttons). The colours of your areas stay your own, so the bloom keeps your meaning while the whole room changes light. Use the switcher above to view any theme on a neutral, white, or dark backdrop.
